Online Presence

21st Century Trust Economy 
2017 the year social media backfired

Online presence is simply what a potential customer will find when they check you out online.

The internet has essentially redefined. how we make our purchasing decisions. We search, we find,, we research and then look for the best value and price. However, the millenial generation added one more decision point. How does my purchase serve the greater good of society. They are focusomg on what has been termed the "HAPPINESS FACTOR". 

The Pew Market Research for 2017 yeilded some more information. People are searching the internet for information but they have a problem "TRUSTING" the information they find.

Look closely at the PEW Market Research chart.  The social media data shows they DON'T trust social media. That is the social media backfire too much of a good thing.

The research shows how people no longer trust social media for making decisions about ANYTHING. People trust family and friends more than social media. That is no surprise. Look closer at the confidence levels which might as well be non-existentent. Social marketers have been mining the web for money. When you add to that the GIMMICK marketing like a celebrity picture used to get you to CLICK-THRU to a sales ad. Dr. Oz said "Something has to be done". 
This .has opened the door for online presence. Business will have to have a complete internet reputation that is verifiable. Your best saleman is your current customer but you have to give them the tools. Facebook can be used as a tool to connect with your current customers.
