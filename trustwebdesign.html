How web design builds trust?

Web design is really about giving hope, building trust for people to take action.
Hope, Trust and Action! These are the 3 elements of a successful web design. I like to call them, the "3 Yes's". Web design satistfies these 3 basic needs.
  1. HOPE the seek factor - A person looking for a product or service is in the seek mode, hoping to find what they are looking for. Try it now!
  2. TRUST - The landing on the website is building the the relationship of trust. It is just like trying a new restaurant and looking at the menu. Try it now!
  3. ACTION - The customer has decided to take action. All the emotional and thought requirements have been met. Try it now!
